Additional Meeting Services

From weddings to conferences, events that set the standard and then continually raise it are part of the success story of the Marriott Hotels brand. At Marriott Resort Palm Jumeirah, we express these capabilities in the refreshing context and setting of a resort, and with the consummate expertise of a seasoned events team. A dedicated events floor offers 11 flexible venues and over 2,200 square meters of space. Nine customizable meeting rooms with high ceilings and natural daylight are versatile and ideal for various event types and styles, and are designed to elevate group thinking while promoting collaborative productivity. A ballroom with floor-to-ceiling windows sets the stage for more distinguished events, including weddings and other joyous celebrations, while its outdoor terrace with limitless vistas of the pool, sea and cityscape add elation to exclusivity.

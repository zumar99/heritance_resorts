ABOUT OUR EXOTIC LUXURY HOTELS IN SRI LANKA


The Heritance brand enriches destinations in Sri Lanka and overseas, bringing into focus the inheritance and culture of each location. Belonging to Aitken Spence Hotels, Heritance Hotels and Resorts is part of the chain of twenty two hotels and resorts that are scattered across South Asia and the Middle East, in Sri Lanka, India, the Maldives, and Oman.

Heritance Hotels and Resorts is a celebration of traditional Sri Lankan heritage and hospitality with pioneer roots in sustainable tourism. The brand has successfully incorporated the convenience of location with the natural wonders in the vicinity, focusing on luxury, quality service and nurturing the local community.

These concepts shine through the design and décor throughout the properties, inspiring guests to explore Sri Lanka through a unique perspective and create lasting memories coloured by the people, places and the stories that they discover.

Open the door to new encounters and indulge in enchanting new journeys with Heritance.

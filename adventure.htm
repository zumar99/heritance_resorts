Beach BBQ Dinner

Enjoy flavourful seafood and a mixed grill BBQ dinner on the beach. Accompanied by the beautiful ocean view, the cool breeze and the sounds of waves, this dinner is a great spot to celebrate with your friends and family.

Location:Heritance Negombo 175 Lewis Pl, Negombo 11500 (https://goo.gl/maps/qj8VjbReZ4SkX7mM9)

Elephant Safari

Ever come so close to an animal, that you felt its warm and intimidating vibe at the same time? Well, here’s your chance. Take a jeep safari to the Minneriya and Kaudulla National Park, and the echoes of trumpets will reach you before you find a herd of elephants crossing the dusty road, while carefully sheltering the calf of the family.

Location:Heritance Kandalama 175 Lewis Pl, Negombo 11500 (https://goo.gl/maps/qj8VjbReZ4SkX7mM9)

Lil’ Pirates Bay Playground

Our Pirate-themed playground is located right next to the park, where parents can play a round of Ping Pong while supervising the kids and having some fun of their own.

Location:Heritance Aarah 175 Lewis Pl, Negombo 11500 (https://goo.gl/maps/qj8VjbReZ4SkX7mM9)
